I have always been intretsted in starting my own clothing brand. I know friends who also started their own clothing brand and it inspired me to do the same. My clothing brand focuses mainly on streetwear fashion. Other examples of popular streetwear clothing brands consist os Fugazi, Unsound and Simplicity. I hope to one day grow as big as them and maybe even work with them. ALthough they arent as big as other clothing brands, I aspire to reach their levek of success. Most of the owners to these streetwear clothing brands are young and undertsand the new streetwear fashion culture. I decided to start my clothing brand now rather than later because the market will later be saturated with alot of other streetwear brands. Right now is still a good time to create your business and grow. My goal is to one day insipre other people to starr their brands.

The name of my streetwear brand is Passenger's Fate. The meaning behind it is too live your life to the fullest extent It's about having control over your own life, and not being the passenger to your own life. The design revolve around a hand holding a plane around the world. With passenger's fate on the top warpped around the earth. It shows alot of people in that plane being controlled and thier fate is not in their hands. Hence the name of Passenger's Fate. It their fate at risk and it shows how poeple are quick to allow other to determine their decisons in life. I want to express the message that you need to control your own life and not rely on others to be happy. The slogan for my clothing brand would be "Dont the the passenger in your own life, decide your own fate."
